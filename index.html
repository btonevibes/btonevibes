The Veil of Ignorance

What if you didn't know who you were?

Imagine stepping behind a Veil of Ignorance. You're about to enter a new society, but here's the catch: you have no idea who you'll be.

You could be anyone – rich or poor, part of a majority or minority, blessed with talent or facing challenges. You simply don't know your gender, ethnicity, social status, or natural abilities.

From this "Original Position," your task is to design the fundamental principles of justice for this society. Since you don't know your own place, you'll naturally want to create rules that are fair and beneficial for everyone, especially the least advantaged.
